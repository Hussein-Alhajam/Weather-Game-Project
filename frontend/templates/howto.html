How to Play

Game Objective

Survive as long as possible by managing your health, hunger, and sanity while exploring the world. Work with other players or compete to gather resources and build a base to protect yourself from threats.

Controls

  • W, A, S, D: Move your character.
  • Mouse Click: Interact with objects.
  • 1, 2, 3: Use inventory slots.
  • Esc: Open game menu.

Stats

  • Health: Represents your life. Avoid losing it completely to stay alive.
  • Hunger: Eat food to maintain energy and avoid starvation.
  • Sanity: Keep your mind stable by avoiding stress-inducing events.

Tips and Tricks

  • Gather resources quickly at the start to build tools and shelter.
  • Keep an eye on your hunger meter and collect food regularly.
  • Avoid staying out at night without a light source.
  • Team up with other players to share resources and defend against threats.
